Awaited 2015

Interested in serving behind the scenes at the Awaited show? Read on.


Awaited is a creative retelling of the Christmas story. This December we’re inviting over 100,000 of our friends and neighbors to experience it. We’re rallying a team of over 4500 volunteers to create a welcoming experience for 30 shows. From parking cars, greeting, serving cookies, hot cocoa and coffee, to ushering and tidying up the building, we need you to connect people to our community through the story that has the power to change us all.

Pick an Awaited Show date below to sign up for a role that fits your schedule and interest. If a date isn’t listed, our roles are filled for that show.
